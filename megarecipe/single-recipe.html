Banana Pancakes

Nutrition:

  • Calories: 200-250 calories
  • Carbohydrates: 40-50 grams
  • Protein: 4-6 grams
  • Fat: 2-4 grams
  • Fiber: 2-4 grams
  • Sugar: 10-15 grams
prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Tinggi Kalori (1) Rendah Lemak (2) Mudah dan Murah (3) Aneka Masakan

instructions

step 1

Mash the Bananas: Peel the ripe bananas and place them in a mixing bowl. Use a fork to mash them until smooth. You can leave a few small lumps for texture if you prefer.

step 2

Add Eggs: Add the eggs to the mashed bananas and mix them together until well combined.

step 3

Combine Dry Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, salt, and ground cinnamon (if using).

step 4

Mix Wet and Dry Ingredients: Pour the banana and egg mixture into the dry ingredients. Stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

step 5

Heat the Pan: Place a non-stick skillet or frying pan over medium heat and add a small amount of butter or oil to prevent sticking.

step 6

Cook Pancakes: Once the pan is hot, pour 1/4 cup portions of the batter onto the pan for each pancake. You can make your pancakes as large or as small as you like. Cook until you see bubbles forming on the surface, which usually takes about 2-3 minutes.

step 7

Flip and Cook: Carefully flip the pancakes and cook the other side for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

step 8

Serve: Remove the pancakes from the pan and keep them warm. You can serve them with your choice of toppings such as maple syrup, fresh berries, chopped nuts, or a dollop of yogurt.

step 9

Enjoy: Serve your banana pancakes while they are still warm and enjoy!

ingredients

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

1/2 cup flax seed meal

1 cup skim milk
